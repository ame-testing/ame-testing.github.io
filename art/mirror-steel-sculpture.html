Mirror Steel Sculpture

My latest sculpture works were conceived as a series of repeating rectangular stainless steel planes with a diagonal fold connecting opposite corners. The polished mirror side produces intersections and layers of images within the sculpture, transforming the order and form of the space it occupies.
